Casualties: Unknown - Tarkov-Style Weapon Mod

Version: 1.0.0.0

A BepInEx mod for Casualties: Unknown Demo that brings Escape from Tarkov-inspired weapons into the game - 13 custom firearms, 2 melee weapons, 9 ammo types, and 9 magazines with a fully integrated caliber isolation system, weighted world spawns, and melee weapon drops.

> **Requires:** CUTarkovMedicalMod must be installed first. This mod has a hard dependency on the medical mod's I18n and console spawn systems.

---

REQUIREMENTS

- Game: Casualties: Unknown Demo (Steam)
- Mod Loader: BepInEx 5.x
- Required Mod: CUTarkovMedicalMod (install first)

Installing BepInEx:
1. Download BepInEx 5.x stable (x64) from https://github.com/BepInEx/BepInEx/releases
2. Extract into your game root folder (Steam/steamapps/common/Casualties Unknown Demo/)
3. Launch the game once to generate the BepInEx/ folder, then close it

---

INSTALLATION

1. Install CUTarkovMedicalMod first
2. Download CUTarkovWeaponMod_v1.0.0.0.zip
3. Extract the contents into your game root folder
   - After extraction:
     [Game Root]/BepInEx/plugins/CUTarkovWeaponMod/
       - CUTarkovWeaponMod.dll
       - Framework/Assets/  (weapon icons + gun sound effects)
       - Lang/              (EN.json, zh_CN.json)
4. Launch the game

Verify: check BepInEx/LogOutput.log for "Casualties: Unknown - Tarkov-Style Weapon Mod loaded."

FEATURES

- 13 custom firearms: bolt-action snipers, semi-auto carbines, full-auto rifles, pump/semi-auto shotguns, pistols, SMG, PDW, LMG
- 2 melee weapons: Red Rebel ice axe (climbing ability) and M-2 tactical sword
- 9 custom ammo types with dedicated calibers
- 9 custom magazines with correct capacity and crafting recipes
- Caliber isolation system: three-layer Harmony patches prevent cross-caliber loading
- Weighted world spawns: 35% pistol / 20% SKS+shotgun / 17% SMG / 13% rifle / 10% sniper / 5% LMG
- Melee weapon drops: 13% chance in supply crates (40% ice axe / 60% M-2 sword)
- Container drops: supply crates (6.6% gun + 10% mag), corpses (3% gun + 3% mag), airdrops (3% gun)
- Gun sound effects: unique fire/bolt/close/mag-in/mag-out sounds per weapon
- Preloaded melee swing sounds for instant playback without I/O lag
- Crafting system: magazines and ammo craftable via recipes
- Vanilla weapon replacement: original weapons/ammo/magazines blocked
- AXMC scope zoom: extended field of view when equipped
- UMP45 built-in suppressor: noise 0.2, muzzle flash disabled
- Console spawn support for all custom items
- Auto-update checker with download, progress bar, and one-click update
- Bilingual: English and Simplified Chinese

CALIBER ISOLATION SYSTEM

Three-layer Harmony patches ensure each firearm only accepts its designated ammo caliber:
- GunScript.LoadMag - magazine-to-gun caliber check
- AmmoScript.LoadRound - ammo-to-magazine caliber check
- AmmoScript.UnloadRound - prevents incorrect ammo unloading

Shared calibers:
- 7.62x39: SKS / AKM / RPD
- 12 gauge: MP-133 / MP-153
- .45 ACP: UMP45 / USP

---

CONSOLE SPAWN

All items spawnable via: spawn [itemkey]
Examples: spawn axmc, spawn akm, spawn 338ucw, spawn akm_mag, spawn redrebel, spawn m2sword, spawn usp

---

AUTO-UPDATE

The mod includes a built-in update checker:
- Automatically checks for new versions on startup
- Two update options: Auto Update (download + install + restart) or Release Page
- Download progress bar with speed indicator
- Cancel download at any time
- Auto-timeout on stalled downloads
- One-click restart after update
